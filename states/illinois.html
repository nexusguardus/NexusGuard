What You Need to Know

  • Illinois uses an OR threshold: exceeding EITHER $100,000 in sales OR 200 transactions triggers nexus.
  • This is one of the lowest thresholds nationally for transaction-based nexus, catching many mid-size sellers.
  • Illinois has a complex tax structure with the Retailers' Occupation Tax (ROT) as its primary sales tax.
  • Home-rule municipalities can impose additional taxes, and combined rates can exceed 10% in Chicago.
